## â“ Troubleshooting

**Q: CC button doesn't work**
A: The video stream may not have captions. Try a different channel.

**Q: PiP button doesn't work on desktop**
A: Your browser may need a recent version. Try Chrome or Firefox.

**Q: Exit button not showing in multiview**
A: Make sure you're actually in multiview mode. Load 4 channels first.

**Q: Buttons fade too quickly**
A: Move your mouse or touch the screen to show HUD for 3 more seconds.

**Q: Buttons look different on mobile**
A: Styling adjusts for smaller screens but functionality is the same.
